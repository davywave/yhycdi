Frequently Asked Questions

  • Volunteering is easy! Fill out our volunteer application form online or visit our center during office hours. We offer various opportunities based on your skills, interests, and availability, including mentoring, event support, administrative tasks, and specialized roles.

  • Our youth development programs primarily serve young people between the ages of 12-24. We have dedicated programs for middle school students (12-14), high school students (15-18), and young adults (19-24). Each program is tailored to meet the specific developmental needs of these age groups.

  • We accept donations through multiple channels: online via our website, by mail (check), through corporate matching programs, planned giving, or in-kind donations. All contributions are tax-deductible. You can also sponsor specific programs or events. Contact our development team for more information.

  • Yes, we offer internships throughout the year for college students and recent graduates interested in youth development, social work, education, nonprofit management, and marketing. Our internships typically last 3-6 months and provide valuable hands-on experience in the nonprofit sector.
